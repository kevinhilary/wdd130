NAIROBI, KENYA

Kenya is a country in East Africa known for its diverse landscapes, wildlife, and rich cultural heritage. Its capital city is Nairobi, a major economic and technological hub in Africa. The country is famous for its natural parks, such s the Masai Maara, beautiful beaches along the indian ocean, and the Great Rift Valley. Kenya's Population is made up of many ethnic groups, each with unique traditions and languages.
