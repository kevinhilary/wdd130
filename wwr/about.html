ROMAN RAFTING COMPANY

The purpose of the company is to create unforgetable adventures that connect people with nature through safe and thrilling rafting experiences. Our motto is "Ride the Rapids. Feel the Freedom." Our mission is to deliver exceptional outdoor experiences by combining expert guidance, safety, and environmental respect, ensuring every guest leaves with lasting memories and a renewed love for the wild. Our creed is to paddle with passion, protect our rivers, and pursue adventure responsibly, flowing together as one team driven by courage, unity and respect for matures power

History

Founded in 2012 along the banks of the Tana River, our rafting company began with a small team of passionate adventurers who shared a dream of bringing people closer to nature through the thrill of white-water rafting. They rafted, drove boughts along the river as they enjoyed themselves through the banks.

What Started as weekend trips for friends soon grew into a trusted outdoor adventure company known for its safety, expertise, and unforgettable river experiences. Over the years, we've experienced our routes, trained local guides, and welcomed thousands of explorers-all while staying true to our love for the river and commitment to sustainable tourism.
